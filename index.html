About AMPERSAND

A Hidden Gem Above the City

Perched above the vibrant streets of downtown Boise, AMPERSAND redefines the art of the cocktail. With no menu, every drink is crafted just for you, tailored to your preferences by our expert mixologists.

This is more than a bar—it’s a place for meaningful connections, quiet indulgence, and moments you’ll savor long after the evening ends. Intimate by design, AMPERSAND is for those who value craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a touch of mystery.
